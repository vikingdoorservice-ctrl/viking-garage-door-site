How Much Does a New Garage Door Cost in South Carolina? (2026)

By the Viking Garage Door crew · Boiling Springs, SC · July 2026

☎ Call (864) 921-7373 Free Estimate

The garage door is up to 30% of your home's face from the street, and it's the biggest moving thing you own. When folks around Spartanburg and Greenville call us about a new one, the first question is always the same: what's this going to run me? Here are real 2026 numbers.

2026 installed prices in the Upstate

  • Single door (8–9 ft wide), non-insulated steel: roughly $900–$1,300 installed.
  • Single door, insulated: $1,100–$1,600.
  • Double door (16×7), non-insulated: $1,400–$1,900.
  • Double door (16×7), insulated steel — our most popular: around $1,950 installed, including haul-away of the old door.
  • Carriage-house style, windows, designer colors: $2,500–$4,000.
  • Full-view glass / modern aluminum: $3,500–$5,500+.

Every real quote depends on measurements and options, which is why we do free on-site estimates — but those ranges are what Upstate homeowners actually pay, not teaser prices.

What moves the price

Insulation is the big one — and in South Carolina it's not about winter, it's about July. An insulated door keeps an attached garage 10–20° cooler in summer, runs quieter, and dents less. Windows add $200–$600 depending on style. Hardware matters more than people think: high-cycle springs and nylon rollers are cheap at install time and expensive to retrofit later.

Questions to ask any installer (including us)

  • Is haul-away of the old door included?
  • Are new tracks and springs included, or reused from the old door?
  • What's the cycle rating on the springs?
  • Is the crew insured, and is the quote final before work starts?

If an installer dodges any of those, keep shopping. We put our answers in writing on every estimate.

Repair first?

One honest note: if your door is structurally sound and the problem is a spring, cable or opener, a repair at $150–$350 is the better money. We tell people that every week. New-door money should buy you looks, insulation and quiet — not just fix a solvable problem.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new garage door cost installed in South Carolina?

In the Spartanburg–Greenville area in 2026: single steel doors typically run $900–$1,500 installed, standard 16×7 insulated double doors around $1,950, and carriage-style or full-view glass doors $3,000–$5,500+.

Does a new garage door increase home value?

Yes — garage door replacement consistently ranks near the top of Remodeling Magazine's Cost vs. Value report, recouping most of its cost at resale, because it changes so much of the home's street view.

How long does garage door installation take?

A standard replacement takes our crew about 3–5 hours including haul-away of the old door. Doors with openers, custom tracks or structural fixes can take longer.
