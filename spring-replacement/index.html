How to Know Your Garage Door Spring Is Broken

  • The door won't open, or the opener strains and gives up
  • You heard a loud bang from the garage
  • The door moves unevenly or slams down fast
  • There's a visible gap in the spring above the door

Do not keep using the door — a broken spring puts the full door weight on the opener and cables and can cause injury. Call us instead.
