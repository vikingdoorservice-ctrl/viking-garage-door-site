While You Wait: Keep It Safe

  • Don't lift a door with a broken spring by hand — it can weigh 150–300 lbs with nothing holding it.
  • Don't pull the red release cord while the door is up — it can slam down.
  • Keep kids, pets and cars away from a door that's crooked or off its track.
  • If the door won't close, unplug the opener so it doesn't grind the motor trying.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you get to me?

Call before noon and we can usually reach you the same day anywhere in Spartanburg County — often within a few hours. Most emergency repairs are completed in 1–2 hours on the first visit.

Yes. A broken spring or snapped cable usually blocks the door — don't force it. We safely release and lift the door, replace the failed part, and have you back on the road the same day.
