Style 01 · Steel

Classic Steel Raised-Panel

The workhorse of the Upstate: durable galvanized steel with the timeless raised-panel look. Low maintenance, dent-resistant, and available insulated for a quieter, more energy-efficient garage.

  • Short or long raised panels
  • White, almond, brown, sandstone & slate gray
  • Non-insulated, R-9 or R-18 insulated cores
  • Optional window sections up top

Style 02 · Modern

Modern Flush & Full-View

Clean, flat panels and black-frame glass that instantly modernize a home. Flush steel for a minimal look, or aluminum-and-glass full-view doors that flood the garage with light.

  • Flush & long-panel steel
  • Black, charcoal & matte finishes
  • Frosted, clear or tinted glass options
  • Bold contemporary curb appeal

Style 03 · Carriage House

Carriage House

The charm of old swing-open barn doors with the convenience of a modern overhead door. Warm tones and detailed panels — optional decorative handles and hinges — for a classic, high-end look.

  • Wood-look & warm painted finishes
  • Optional decorative hardware
  • Arched or square window inserts
  • Great fit for craftsman & farmhouse homes
